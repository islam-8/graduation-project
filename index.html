Core Research Problem

The Granularity Gap

Conventional air-defense was engineered for supersonic missiles with RCS > 5m². Today's micro-drones operate at σ < 0.01m² — collapsing radar detection probability from ~80% down to barely 12% and vanishing into Doppler notch filters when hovering.

This project bridges that gap through optical-primary detection with 25× zoom and a YOLOv8n INT8 edge AI pipeline running on commercial off-the-shelf hardware at a fraction of the cost of conventional C-UAS solutions.

Concept of Operations

System Workflow

1

Surveillance

Autonomous PTZ scanning or RF-triggered Slew-to-Cue. Hikvision DarkFighter 25× zoom covers 360° continuously, day and night.

2

AI Identification

YOLOv8n INT8 on Raspberry Pi 5 processes 30 FPS in real time. Reliable Drone / Bird / Background discrimination — mAP@50: 0.888.

3

Kinetic Lock

Discrete Kalman Filter + PID controller maintains target centroid. Direct ISAPI injection achieves sub-10ms PTZ command latency.

4

Operator Alert

Encrypted Telegram alerts with JPEG snapshots over 4G. Remote ARM / DISARM via bot commands. Mean alert delay: 1.2 seconds.
